About Pathibhara Hotel

Our Story

Established in 2005, Pathibhara Hotel has been serving authentic Nepali cuisine to locals and tourists alike. Named after the sacred Pathibhara temple, we take pride in offering traditional flavors with a modern touch.

Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods to bring you the true taste of Nepal. From our famous momos to our hearty thukpa, every dish tells a story of our rich culinary heritage.

Whether you're looking for a quick snack or a full meal with friends and family, Pathibhara Hotel promises an unforgettable dining experience.
